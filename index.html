About me

Hello, this is Mashiat! I am currently working as an Adjunct Lecturer at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering of Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology. I completed my Bachelors degree from the same department this May, 2023 and currently enrolled in the Masters Program here.

Currently I am looking for research opportunities in the field of Computer Graphics. This is the course I enjoyed the most during my bachelors degree but till now, couldn't find any proper research in this field in my country.

During my bachelors degree, I did my thesis in Bioinformatics under the supervision of Dr.Shamsuzzoha Bayzid on building a prediction model for Alzheimer's Disease based on genetic data. Over the last four years, I participated in multiple competitions in the field of Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Object Detection, Robot Programming etc and won in a few. Our idea on AI Based Dengue Control & Monitor system won 2nd prize in Microsoft Azure Hackathon and UNDP Hackathon.

Besides my academics, I enjoy travelling, reading books, dancing, and specially charity work. I co-lead a project named Library Project in an NGO called Agami Education Foundation. Our project started in 2019 and till now we have reached around 2000 underprivileged students aiming to improve their reading fluency and increasing their reading habit. I love to organize and lead people and have been involved in many extra curricular activities since my childhood.
